Why does this website exist?

With the European Broadcasting Union allowing Israel to participate in the 2026 Eurovision Song Contest, many people have chosen to boycott this year's contest. 5 participating countries have even withdrawn.

Not everyone, however, is willing to suddenly let go of a contest they've watched for years and that they're huge fans of. And I understand that.
But we've still got to let the EBU know that it allowing Israel to participate is unacceptable. And since they obviously haven't cared about the immense criticism and disrepute they've faced these past few months, we've got to hit them where it hurts: TV ratings. And that's why I made this website.

By watching Eurovision through the unofficial livestreams of the national broadcasters' channels on this page, you can still watch the contest without contributing to the broadcasters' ratings.

Disclaimer / DMCA

This website only hosts links to publicly accessible livestreams of free-to-air TV channels. They aren't hosted by the website owner¹, who can't be sure of their real origin and is assuming in good faith they haven't been illegally obtained. Either way, if you represent a broadcaster featured on this website and would like your relevant stream(s) removed, email dmca@escfent.com.

¹ The streams on the escfent.com domain, as their subdomains suggest, are merely reverse proxies of IP addresses of third-party streaming servers the website owner doesn't own and has no control of. The reason the IPs are proxied through the escfent.com domain is to make their server's streams securely accessible via SSL.
